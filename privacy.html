TfL Delay Repay

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

TfL Sign-in

TfL Delay Repay opens TfL's website inside an in-app browser so you can sign in directly. Your TfL credentials are entered directly on TfL's site — this app never sees, stores, or transmits your username or password.

Journey Data

Your journey history is fetched from TfL using your authenticated session and stored on your device only. It is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data We Collect

No personal data is collected by us. All journey data, delay claims, and app settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-party Services

This app uses no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
